Instructions for use

Deposit FAQ

1. The payment address is a one-time address. Users need to obtain a new payment address each time they make a deposit. Repeated deposits to the same payment address are not allowed.

2. The deposit will automatically arrive in your account within 15 minutes. If delayed, please contact customer service with payment voucher.

3. Cryptocurrency transfer times vary by region and wallet (may exceed 72 hours). Contact customer service after TXID generation.

4. Only USDT is accepted. Other currencies (TRX/TUSD/BUSD) will cause recharge failure and fund loss!

Withdrawal FAQ

1. Withdrawals arrive within 24 hours (average 30 minutes). Time varies by network congestion.

2. New users must bind withdrawal info immediately after registration.

3. Withdrawal information cannot be modified frequently. Confirm wallet selection carefully.
